==About==
A backpack has 20 inventory slots. When an item is placed in a backpack its weight is reduced by 50%.

To open the backpack right click on its icon when you equip it.

Items in your backpack are not saved when you get KO'd.

When you are using hotkeys like shift+right click, items from backpack are managed separately.

When crafting or building, the game will '''''not''''' recognize items in your backpack for use as inputs/ingredients available.</text>
