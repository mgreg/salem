==About==

Sent in the mail on February 28, 2013 to all active accounts when Closed Beta became Open Beta.   One pack per account was awarded.

In the time since then, the server was reset so that no Plymouth Rock Armor sets exist in Salem.  A few isolated pieces have been handed out to players by the developers, roses were sold in the Seasonal Stall for a time, and the hat can now be found in the Mystery Hat Box, but there is yet no word on whether the full set will ever be redistributed.
