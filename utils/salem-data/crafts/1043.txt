==About==
[[File:Lighting_Fire.png|thumb|right|Shows a character after using the Tinder Drill in an attempt to light a [[Pile of Wood]].]]
A Tinder Drill is used to start fires in any lightable object, such as: [[Torch|Torches]], a [[Pile of Wood]], [[Fireplace]]s, [[Meat Smoker]]s, and others.

Tinder Drills are consumed when they start a fire, and also have a chance to break before a fire is successfully lit. That chance decreases as your [[Sparks &amp; Embers]] proficiency increases

Using a Tinder Drill drains [[Phlegm]], and, if it breaks during use, it damages the users [[Blood]].

To use a Tinder Drill, merely pick it up and right click the item to light.

It's suggested to keep two or three of these on hand just in case of one breaking.

Tinder drills are very slow and unreliable at starting fires. Most players use tinder drills to light a [[fireplace]] or [[stove]] and keep that fed through their play sessions.  For additional fires, a [[Torch]] can be used.

Very rarely, you may get a [[Precious Soot-Flake]] when lighting a fire with a Tinder Drill

Players who can make shop purchases may want to consider [[Flint &amp; Steel]].</text>
