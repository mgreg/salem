==About==

The Flintlock Pistol is a ranged weapon that requires [[Gunpowder]] and [[Musket Balls]] to fire.  To attack with a pistol in combat, you must use [[Shoot]]. It fires in a straight like in the direction the player is facing. Shots do not travel past solid objects.

Pistols can be dual wielded and are slightly faster than Muskets.  The damage of a Pistol is half that of Muskets, but as a Musket takes both hands to wield, some prefer Pistols.

*Can be bought in [[Providence]] for 350 [[Silver Pieces]].
