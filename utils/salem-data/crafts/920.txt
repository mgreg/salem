==About==
[[Golden Corn]] is the 3rd-tier variety of [[Corn]].  It is produced from a field whose total accumulated influence from [[Cabbage]] and [[Cotton]] is above 150. 

This kind of Corn is required for the [[Goldtilla]].

Due to the fact that Cotton and Cabbage drain each other's influence bars, getting them both high at the same time is somewhat...''challenging'', making Golden Corn difficult to obtain (same as any other Tier 3 crop).</text>
