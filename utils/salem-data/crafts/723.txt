==About==
Has to be cleaned with water to make a plant fibre.  This can be done by right-clicking a [[Bucket of Water]] on it (uses 1 liter), or by right-clicking the charred fiber in water such as a lake or stream.

When washing [[Charred Milkweed Fibre]], there is a chance of producing a [[Precious Soot-Flake]].

Milkweed Jute will retain the original purity of the [[Milkweed Roots]] after being charred and washed.  Since Milkweed Root is pottable, it can be a useful way to get [[Purity]] [[Fish Trap]]s if your Garden purity is higher than your Cotton purity.</text>
