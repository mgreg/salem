==About==
Can be used to replace a [[Stone]], whenever a [[Stone]] is needed.&lt;br/&gt;
Also acts as a poor [[Agriculture/Fertilizers &amp; Field Values|Fertilizer]].


'''Fertilizer Uses:'''

-- Without Three-Field System, Fertilizer 1 = First fertilizer to be used on field since seeds were planted; for each fertilizer you used on the field (regardless of which kind) add that to determine which fertilizer # your adding; Example: If you put (1) wood chip on the field, then used this fertilizer, you would get fertilizer 2 as the 2nd fertilizer used on the field.

The formula follows 10/sqrt(x) (for speed and plenty), so:

Fertilizer 1: +10% Speed, -10% Plenty

Fertilizer 2: +7% Speed, -7% Plenty

Fertilizer 3: +5% Speed, -5% Plenty

Fertilizer 4: +5% Speed, -5% Plenty

Fertilizer 5: +4% Speed, -4% Plenty

Fertilizer 6: +4% Speed, -4% Plenty

Fertilizer 7: +3% Speed, -3% Plenty

Fertilizer 8: +3% Speed, -3% Plenty

Fertilizer 9: +3% Speed, -3% Plenty

Fertilizer 10: +3% Speed, -3% Plenty



NOTE: No stats can fall bellow 50%, once your plenty hits 50% you will not be able to use any more dross until you raise your plenty.

TOTAL GAIN IN 10 USES: +49% Speed, -49% Plenty 



''Recommended Use: Typically if you're starting out new farms, you use cabbage seeds to plant the 100% upkeep fields then pile dross on to decrease unwanted colewort and increase speed; then drop the upkeep to replant with desired product! Mildly Effective''
