==About==
An intermediary resource acquired by chipping away at a [[Copper Ore Boulder]].

[[Bar of Copper]] is smelted from this by putting it into an [[Ore Smelter]], usually accompanied by [[Lime]].

Ore can '''''not''''' be used as a replacement for [[stone]].

For the full process, see [[Mining]].</text>
