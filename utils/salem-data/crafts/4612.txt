==About==

Used to be called Bar of Iron.

*See [[Ore Smelter]] for results of ore refinement. 
*Can be bought in Boston for 200 [[Silver Pieces]] each.
*Because the [[Ore Smelter]] requires iron bars to build it, players are expected to buy at least these first three bars in order to begin smelting ore.

Bar of Pig Iron transfers its [[Purity]] to [[Sword Blade]] and [[Knife Blade]].</text>
