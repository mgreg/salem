==About==

Hay is also a byproduct of harvesting [[Ear of Cereal|Ears of Cereal]], and will share the purity of the harvested grain.  A third way to make Hay is to build and supply a [[Haystack]] with raw materials.

'''Ferilizer Uses:'''

-- Without Three-Field System, Fertilizer 1 = First fertilizer to be used on field since seeds were planted; for each fertilizer you used on the field (regardless of which kind) add that to determine which fertilizer # your adding; Example: If you put (1) wood chip on the field, then used this fertilizer, you would get fertilizer 2 as the 2nd fertilizer used on the field.

==Formula==
The formula follows '''50/sqrt(x)''', so:

Fertilizer 1: +50% Influence

Fertilizer 2: +35% Influence 

Fertilizer 3: +28% Influence 

Fertilizer 4: +25% Influence 

Fertilizer 5: +22% Influence

Fertilizer 6: +20% Influence

Fertilizer 7: +18% Influence

Fertilizer 8: +17% Influence

Fertilizer 9: +16% Influence

Fertilizer 10: +15% Influence

TOTAL GAIN IN 10 USES: +251% Influence

==Three-field System==
[[Three-field System]] provides a flat bonus to the Influence-gain for each use of the fertilizer. This bonus is equal to 1/5th of the Base Value.
*Hay's bonus is +10.</text>
