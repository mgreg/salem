==About==

Very rare drop from killing Rabbits and March Hares. It is better to sell this item rather than use it as an Inspirational.

Not only is this an Inspirational, it can also be used in Combat to give the player who uses it a boost of speed.  Running speed is greatly increased for several seconds when using this item.  This also has the effect of helping cancel out a snare if one has been applied.
