==About==

[[Corn Meal]] is produced from milling [[Seeds of Corn]] in a [[Windmill]], a slower [[Copper Grinder]] or a much slower [[Grindstone]].  There are 4 kinds of Cornmeal, depending on the variety of [[Corn]] milled:

*[[Yellow Cornmeal]]
*[[White Cornmeal]]
*[[Blue Cornmeal]]
*[[Golden Cornmeal]]

Recipes that call for [[Any Cornmeal]] can, unsurprisingly, use any of these.  You will usually use [[Yellow Cornmeal]] though, because it is the easiest to come by.

==Yield==

50 Seeds of Corn produce 2.00 Kg of Cornmeal.  (A corn field at 100% Plenty produces 50 seeds)</text>
