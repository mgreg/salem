==About==

Isolated somewhere, deep in the tall grass, rifle steadied for the past half hour waiting. Watching. The hunter is ever vigilant in the hopes of a catch to put meat on the table and earn a trophy for the wall. Smelling of smoked meat and the open forest, this pack contains everything the budding woodsman needs to master his art.
