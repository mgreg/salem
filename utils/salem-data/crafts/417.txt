Left-click on [[Lime]] to pick it up, then right-click with it on a [[Bucket of Water]] to produce a bucket of [[Milk of Lime]].

The ''concentration'' of the Milk of Lime depends on how many times you add a [[Lime]] to it, starting at 1% and a max of 5%.</text>
