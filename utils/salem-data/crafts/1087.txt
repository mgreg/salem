==About==

Obtained inside a Turkey Coop with Turkey Hen or Turkey Gobbler and food for them.

Good Fertilizer or can be used for compost. 

For those seeking speed and influence, alternating between [[Hay]] and [[Charcoal]] gives more speed and influence than using turkey droppings. The real benefit of Turkey Droppings is that it reduces upkeep by 5 when used as the first fertilizer on a field, exactly the amount of upkeep the field is reduced by each planting.  Great for those who aren't planning to fertilize a great deal.
