==About==
[[Corn Oil]] is created by pressing [[Corn]] in a [[Press]].  Specific oils, [[Yellow Corn Oil]], [[Blue Corn Oil]], [[White Corn Oil]], and [[Golden Corn Oil]] can all be created from their respective seeds as long as there is no other oil in the Press to mix with it.

[[Corn Oil]] as a variable ingredient is called for in a few recipes, but can also be used as a generic [[Vegetable Oil]] anywhere Oil itself is needed.

Pressing [[Corn]] yield oil at a rate of 1.00 Liters for every 20 seeds, or 5.00 Liters for a full stack.  A field of Corn at 100% plenty yields 50 seeds.</text>
