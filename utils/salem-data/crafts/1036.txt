*Acquired by drying an [[Raw Argopelt]] for 24 hours.

It can be used to craft [[Argopelter Trophy]] which can be sold in [[Providence]] to NPC vendors for 135 silver coins.  Or just sell the dried pelt itself to the NPC.
You can also hang the Trophy on your wall.</text>
