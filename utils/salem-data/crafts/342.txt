==About==
Used for crafting Pots, Urns and Bricks, as well as a few inspirationals.  Making Pots/Urns/Bricks requires the use of a [[Kiln]].

*When obtaining clay, there is a chance of producing a [[Misshapen Lump of Clay]]


==Fertilizer==
Can also be used as a terrible, ''terrible'' fertilizer.

'''Stats:''' -10% Speed, +10% Influence.

After 10 uses, the field will be at: 50% Speed, 150% Influence. 

The field will take 2x as long to mature, but only yield 1.5x the influence.  This is ''75% as effective as '''doing nothing'''''.</text>
