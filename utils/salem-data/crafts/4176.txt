==About==

Whetsones are obtained rarely when mining boulders or stones. 

The Whetstone, when mined from a boulder, will have the same purity as that boulder.  With it, you can sharpen a store bought Sword type weapon and the sword will take on the purity of the Whetstone. In this way, swords can have [[Purity]] which increase the amount of damage done when doing [[Thrust]].

A whetstone can only be used on store bought swords, such as [[Adventurer's Rapier]], [[Claymore]], [[Nobleman's Rapier]], [[Nobleman's Rapier]], or [[Pirate Captain's Sword]], as the [[Spanish Sabre]] takes on the purity on the [[Bar of Pig Iron]] it was crafted with.

Beware, this counts as [[Any Stone]] in recipes and can also be paved.
