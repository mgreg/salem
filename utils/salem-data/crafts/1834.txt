==About==

[[Image:Mereni.png|thumb|right|Preferable by women-folk, but an excellent choice of disguise when burglarizing an orphanage.]]

A clothing item that also doubles as an inpsirational.  This is one of several items that can have a different hidden outcome if you replace one of the ingredients in crafting.  For example it can be used with a [[March Hare Hide]] in place of the [[Rabbit Skin]] to create a [[Bunny Darko Masque]].

==Flavor Text==
''A toy for boys at play, and gals of lush delights.''
