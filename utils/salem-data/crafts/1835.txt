==About==

Substituting [[March Hare Hide]] for [[Rabbit Skin]] when crafting [[Bunny Masque]] will create this [[Inspirationals|inspirational]] instead.  This inspirational is a play on the cult-favorite movie Donny Darko and make even further references to it in the flavor text.

==Flavor Text==
''Ever since you first laid eyes upon him, he has begun appearing to you in your dreams, whispering wicked tales of grim portents and dark futures yet to happen.''
