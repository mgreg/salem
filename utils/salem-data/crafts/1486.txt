==About==
Once harvested [[Raw Cotton]] is [[Clean Cotton|cleaned]] to produce [[Cotton]] and [[Seeds of Cotton]].

The [[Cotton]] can then be weaved into [[Cotton Cloth]].
The type of cotton produced is dependent on the tier of the [[Raw Cotton]] used.
