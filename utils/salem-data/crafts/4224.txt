==About==

Candy Baskets were given out as part of the [[Halloween]] 2014 events and are not available only by purchasing from other players.

Opening one gives a random number of special Halloween candies which act as restore foods.  The minimum number of candies to get from a basket is 2.  The max is unknown, but amounts as high as 10 were rarely obtained.</text>
