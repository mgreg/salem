==About==
[[Garlic]] are plants which can only be raised in a [[Gardening Pot]].&lt;br/&gt;
If you do not already have a [[Garlic]] plant to place in a pot you will instead need to plant a [[Wild Garlic]] which has a random chance of resulting in [[Wild Garlic]], or [[Garlic]].
Six Garlic can be used to craft a [[Garlic Braid]] inspirational.


[[Garlic]] can be replanted just like other [[Potable|Potable Plants]]. Although this may be less efficient than trying to use a [[Sprouted Garlic Clove]], you don't need to wait for it to sprout.</text>
