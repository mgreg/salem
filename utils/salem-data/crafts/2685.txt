The Founding Father's Arquebus is a ranged weapon once available from the [[Founding Father's Pack]] and now found in the [[Mystery Weapon Chest]]. It cannot be crafted. Can only be equipped when [[Firearms]] has been learned.  Guns can only be shot when the maneuver [[At the Ready]] is enabled.  Players must with the mouse and use [[Shoot]] to fire.  Requires preparation in the use of [[Gunpowder]] and [[Musket Balls]].


Can be hung on a wall.
