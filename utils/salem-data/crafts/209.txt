Woodblocks are a crafting material that can be chopped from [[log]]s, [[Hollow Log|hollow logs]] or from woodpiles found in forests. An [[axe]] is required to chop wood. You can also get Woodblocks by destroying blackberry bushes, thorn bushes, myrtle oak trees, tree stumps, and hollow stumps.&lt;br/&gt;

It costs 0.1-0.2 [[Phlegm]] for each Woodblock cut from a log.  Chopping wood blocks from a woodpile is significantly faster, however woodpiles are non-renewable.
