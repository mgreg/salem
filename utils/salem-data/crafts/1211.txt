==About==

Can by harvested by butchering dead animals. [[Butcher's Thrift]] allows more Meat Shreds to be retrieved from each carcass.

You can split [[Any Raw Meat Cut]] into 2 [[Meat Shreds]].

==Variable Bonus==

The shreds of each kind of [[Any Raw Meat Cut]] are unique from one another when used in crafting recipes.  For example, using three [[Meat Shreds]] butchered from a Rabbit in a [[Lumberjack Frikadel]] will yield a different crafting bonus for [[Gluttony]] events than by using shreds from a Bear or other source.  

Note that if you use shreds from two different sources, you will not get either bonus!</text>
