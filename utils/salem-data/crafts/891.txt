&quot;Flour&quot; is a generic term which represents four different types of flour available in the game and which is crafted by grinding the [[Ear of Cereal]]s produced from Cereal fields in a [[Windmill]] or in a slower [[Copper Grinder]] or much slower [[Grindstone]]. 

Any recipe which states &quot;Flour&quot; as ingredient allows you to use any tier of flour, and each type will create a food with different properties.

The different tiers of flour available are:

{| border=&quot;2&quot; style=&quot;width: 770px;&quot;
! Tier 1 !! Tier 2 !! Tier 4
|-
| style=&quot;vertical-align: top; text-align: center;&quot;| [[Oatmeal]]
| style=&quot;vertical-align: top; text-align: center;&quot;| [[Rye Flour]]&lt;br/&gt;[[Barley Flour]]
| style=&quot;vertical-align: top; text-align: center;&quot;| [[Wheat Flour]] 
|-
|}

One [[inventory]] slot can hold up to 0.50 kg of Flour.
One [[Ear of Cereal]] can produce up to 2.0 kg of Flour.

 
Flour inherits the purity of the [[Ear of Cereal]] it is created from, combining different batches of flour in a [[Barrel]] or [[Dry Goods Bag]] averages the purity and cannot be reversed. Mixing different types of flour results in generic &quot;Flour&quot; and cannot be separated.</text>
