==About==

Acquired by murdering a player and using 'Scalp' on the body, this gruesome trophy is the skin and hair of a deceased player.  Those who harvest the scalps of their victims are often so enthusiastic, the top of the skull is removed completely and the brain is visible.

Scalps may be saved as trophies or turned in to [[Mr. Puggles]] for a possible reward.</text>
