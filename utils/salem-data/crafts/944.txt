[[Cabbage Seedstalk]] will turn into 20 Seeds of Cabbage after ~48 hours left in any container.

You can also buy 15 Seeds of Cabbage in Boston for 75 [[Silver Pieces]] or better from other players.

You need 15 Seeds of Cabbage to plant a [[Tilled Field]] with 100% Upkeep.

Seed [[Purity]] is one of the three factors that influences Harvest Purity.</text>
