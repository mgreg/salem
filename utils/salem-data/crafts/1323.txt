==About==

These seeds allow you to grow cereals which are used to produce various tiers of [[Flour]].  A [[Tilled Field]] prepared with [[Humus]] requires 50 Seeds of Cereal in order to plant the field.

[[Providence]] sells 50 Seeds of Cereal for 125 [[Silver Pieces]].

You can also obtain Seeds of Cereal after harvesting the cereals by using the {{PropVals/Print Entry|showamount=no|Thresh Cereals}} crafting option.  This consumes the cereal however so you cannot use that one to produce [[Flour]].

Requires the [[Agriculture]] skill to plant, not a particular skill like the other crops.

Seed [[Purity]] is one of the three factors that influences Harvest Purity.</text>
