==About==

Can be sold to the stalls in [[Providence]] for 22 [[Silver]].  This is one of the more reliable ways to make early game money.  The other way being searching for [[Indian Feathers]] to make [[Savage Charms]].

When leveling [[Flora &amp; Fauna]] or [[Hunting &amp; Hideworking]], it is more efficient to just study the dead [[Dead Wartbite Cricket|crickets]].

==Flavor Text==

In lieu of its ongoing campaign to improve living conditions in the New World, the Hudson Bay Company offers a standing bounty on any team of crickets presented before it.
