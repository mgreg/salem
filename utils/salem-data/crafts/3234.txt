==About==

Reward from [[Mr. Puggles]] for turning in a top 10 [[Scalp]], cannot be crafted. Be careful when wearing these since most players will try and kill you just for your clothes.

Can also be mounted on a wall.

The Executioner's Mask is the only slottable item that can be equiped on the [[Face]] slot.
