==About==
* Stone is an item that can be obtained from [[Stone Boulder]]s lying around on the ground, right-click on the rock and select Chip Stone.
* You need the [[Colonial Tradesmanship]] skill in order to unlock the Chip Stone command
* You need an Axe or Pickaxe to chip stone.
* Note: [[Dross]], [[Lime]], [[Flint]], [[Granite]] and [[Smooth Stone]] may all substitute for Stone in many recipes too, so be careful as the game may inadvertently choose one of those items for crafting or building.
