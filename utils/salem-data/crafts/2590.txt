==About==
Once obtained from [[Redcoat's Pack]], now available from the [[Mystery Weapon Chest]].  Cannot be crafted.

[[File:Redcoat's Pack Image.png|thumb|right|alt=Male character wearing Redcoat's pack.|Character wearing Recoat's Pack.]]


The Redcoat's Musket is a ranged weapon that requires gunpowder and musket balls to fire.  To attack with a musket in combat, you must use [[Shoot]]. It fires in a straight like in the direction the player is facing. Shots do not travel past solid objects.
