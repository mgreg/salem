==About==

Sprouted Garlic Cloves can be replanted for more garlic.
Sprouted Garlic Cloves can be obtained by letting a regular Garlic Clove sit around somewhere for around 1-2 days.

Once a Regular Garlic Clove has sprouted it can no longer be used as a Regular Clove for recipes.

When planted will result in 1 Garlic without [[Horticulture]], and 2 Garlics when you have the skill.</text>
