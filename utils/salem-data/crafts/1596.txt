==About==

Early crafted inspirational. One of the first sources of [[Cloak &amp; Dagger]].  Because this inspirational requires both [[Stray Chestnut]] and [[Waxing Toadstool]] it is highly advisable that you have early access to an [[Autumnal]] [[Biome]] to craft these.

==Flavor Text==
''A taste of rotting leaves, and of life dying to live again.''</text>
