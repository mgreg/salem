==About==

Early crafted inspirational.  Easily made while still in Boston.  Flint is most certainly the most &quot;rare&quot; of the 4 ingredients.  When doing early-game foraging once you find several [[Flint]] in your inventory you can move the nearest [[Stone Boulder]] to a [[Tree]] and quickly gather the other three ingredients all in or near the same location.

==Flavor Text==
''A taste of salt, and of seas, and of murmuring woods undisturbed by the ages.''</text>
