==About==
The Slingshot is the first Ranged Weapon available in the game.  Although the [[Plant Fibre]] required in its construction can be troublesome to obtain, it is a useful tool for hunting, since it allows you to initiate combat without the animal running away.

The Slingshot uses [[Any Stone|stones]] as ammunition.  A stone of any kind must be in your inventory in order to fire the Slingshot.

*'''Caution:''' The Slingshot will happily fire [[Smooth Stone]]'s, [[Lime]], and [[Flint]] at your target, so be careful that you don't end up shooting valuable stones.

To Fire a Slingshot, you must first select the [[Shoot]] command from the Combat-&gt;Attack menu.  Clicking with your mouse-cursor will aim the shot at wherever you clicked.  Your character will grasp the slingshot in their Left hand while pulling back with their Right.  The right-hand will shift around a bit while &quot;taking aim&quot;, but this should not be confused with swinging a Sling (as opposed to a Slingshot) around in the air with your Right hand, since Slings are not in the game.

When the shot is fired, a white line will trace it's trajectory to the target, and the fist-sized rock you just hurled will do a trivial amount of damage and serve only to make the creature angry.  Be prepared to engage in combat now, since a homicidal bunny rabbit (Or whatever you attacked) will be barreling down at you, thirsting for blood.  if you fired from maximum range, you should have time to perform a [[Stomp]], but it's safer to be ready with a more direct attack instead, like [[Punch]] or [[Thrust]].

==Notes==
* A Slingshot MUST be equipped to the Left Hand.
* Slingshot damage seems to be around 0.5*sqrt(YB/10).
* Common Combat Power [[Artifact]]s increase the damage done by a Slingshot, but the difference is not enough to matter.
* Impassable objects (walls, boulders, stumps...) cannot be shot over.
* The slingshot targets the ground, not the animal you are trying to hit.  Aim for it's feet and you'll hit fine.
