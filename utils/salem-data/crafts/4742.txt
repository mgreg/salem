==About==

Can be be found sometimes when Rummaging in an [[Abandoned Bird's Nest]].

Can be cracked open while having an empty liquid vessel in your inventory (not backpack) and will result in [[Raw Egg Yolks]]. Drinking it will give the [[Mighty Muscless]] buff (MM will temporarily increase your max carry weight.).

Sparrow Eggs gives 0.05L of Raw Egg Yolks.</text>
