==About==

Whenever you harvest a [[Tilled Field|Field]], there is a chance of getting Wild Oats.  The [[Purity]] of Wild Oats is equal to that of the seeds used to plant the field, not the product returned from the field.

When harvested, Wild Oats are the first item to appear in your inventory before any Crops or [[Hay]].</text>
