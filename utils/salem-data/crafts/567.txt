==About==
When you harvest a [[cotton]] field you will obtain several units of [[Raw Cotton]] in your [[inventory]] depending on your field's level of ''Plenty'' (see [[Farming]]).&lt;br/&gt;
You may then process the [[Raw Cotton]] using the Crafting menu: {{PropVals/Print Entry|showamount=no|Craft}} &amp;rArr; {{PropVals/Print Entry|showamount=no|Processing &amp; Materials}} &amp;rArr; {{PropVals/Print Entry|showamount=no|Clean Cotton}} to separate each unit into 1 [[Clean Cotton]] and 10 units of [[Seeds of Cotton]].


The meter levels of the field determines the tier of Cotton harvested (see [[Agriculture]]).
