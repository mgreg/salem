==About==
[[Vegetable Oil]] is a generic term for [[Oil]] made from vegetable sources, and the name given to oils which have been mixed together. Mixing seed types in the [[Press]] will produce a non-specific &quot;Vegetable Oil&quot;, as will mixing Oils in a [[Barrel]] or [[Bucket]], or [[Oiling Trough]].

If you need a specific kind of oil, like [[Corn Oil]], be careful not to accidentally mix it with other kinds.

==Types==
There are currently 4 kinds of Oil in the game:
*[[Vegetable Oil]] (mixed oils)
*[[Corn Oil]]: 100 seeds = 5L
*[[Cottonseed Oil]]: 100 seeds = 10L
*[[Pumpkinseed Oil]]: 100 seeds = 7.5L

Using less than a full stack of seeds will simply result in that fraction of oil being produced. 20 [[Seeds of Corn]] will give you 1 Liter of [[Corn Oil]] when pressed, and 80 [[Seeds of Pumpkin]] will give you 6 Liters of [[Pumpkinseed Oil]].</text>
