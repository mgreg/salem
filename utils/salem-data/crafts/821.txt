==About==

[[Green Bell Peppers]] and Red Bell Peppers are plants which can only be raised in a [[Gardening Pot]]. If you do not already have a pepper to place in a pot you will instead need to plant a [[New World Gourd]] which has a random chance of resulting in either [[Baby Bear]] (a type of pumpkin), [[Green Bell Peppers]] or Red Bell Peppers.

Not a good Gluttony food at all but great when used as a Variable Ingredient.
