==About==
Resource acquired by chipping away at a [[Rubble Boulder]].

[[Sift Rubble]] to get various resources like: [[Uvarovite]], [[Rubellite]], [[Dravite]], [[Phosphophyllite]], [[Black Sand]], [[Goldflakes]].

Each piece of Rubble has a Hardness based on chance and the [[Mines &amp; Mountains]] of the player who chippied it. That Hardness will transfer over onto any Gemstone which is found in that rubble. See [[Gemcutter's Wheel]] for more information.</text>
