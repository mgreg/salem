==About==
*Random chance to obtain through [[Digging]];
*Random chance to find in [[Compost Bin]];
*Can be found in Old Stumps;
*Counts as +1 compost if added to a [[Compost Bin]] and +10 for [[Turkey Coop]];
*Can be used as a Lure for [[fishing]].

==Purity==

Worms can have their purity adjusted by feeding them plants with the purity you want them to eventually obtain. You must place the worm in a container and then place an item of the [[Any Plant]] category immediately next to the worm. After a few hours the worm will consume that plant and the worm's alchemy levels will make a 10% adjustment towards the plant's alchemy. For example if the plant had 40 AEther value and the worm had 30 AEther value, once the worm feeds on the plant it will gain 10% of the difference which is 1 and end up having 31 AEther value.

Worms pass their Purity directly to the Humus they produce in a bin.  Humus is one of three components for Gardening Purity.
