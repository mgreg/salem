==About==

Fresh Boards are obtained by placing a [[Log]] on a [[Sawbuck]] and using a Saw to Make Boards by right clicking on the loaded Sawbuck.  A fresh Log will produce 30 boards.  A [[Coffin Plank]] will sometimes be created rather than a Fresh Board.  Be careful to set it aside right away so you don't use it as a board.

A [[Timber Pile]] must be built to turn Fresh Boards to [[Dry Board]]s and from there into [[Planed Board]]s or [[Oiled Board]]s.

Any Recipe that uses a [[Board]] can also use a Fresh Board.</text>
