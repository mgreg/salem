==About==

Obtained randomly from turning [[dirt]] into [[clay]] with a [[Clay Trough]]. They can be found digging standard clay nodes as well.

Can be burnt in a [[Kiln]], giving it a chance to turn into [[The Golem of Prague]].

Can still be used as [[clay]] in construction signs.

==Flavor Text==
''However much you mold and shape this strange lump, it seems always to return to its twisted form, as if yearning for a new becoming.''</text>
