==About==

Rye Flour is tier 2 [[Flour]] and can be made using player built [[Windmill]]s, [[Copper Grinder]]s, or [[Grindstone]]s.

One [[Inventory]] slot can hold up to 0.50 kg of Rye Flour.
One [[Ear of Rye]] can produce up to 2.0 kg of Rye Flour.


Rye Flour is a type of [[Flour]] hence may be used for any recipe requiring generic [[Flour]].</text>
