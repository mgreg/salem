==About==
[[Milkweed Roots]] take about 20 minutes to bake after being placed in a [[Fireplace]].

They can then either be used to make [[Froghetti]] or washed with water (from a bucket or in a stream) to produce [[Milkweed Jute]], an important early source of [[Plant Fibre]].

When washing Charred Milkweed Fibre, there is a chance of producing a [[Precious Soot-Flake]].</text>
