==About==

One should use extreme caution before turning all their [[Earthworm Pythons]] into these.   Pythons give more [[Cloak &amp; Dagger]] and [[Thread &amp; Needle]] separately than this finished product.  Be sure to look at the proficiency you are attempting to get and make sure the concordant is better than the pythons individually.

It also instantly fills a [[Turkey Coop]].
