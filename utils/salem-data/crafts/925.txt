==About==
&quot;Head of Cabbage&quot; is a generic term which represents all the different tiers of cabbage available in the game. They are created by harvesting Cabbage Fields. In recipes, leaves of colewart do not count as &quot;any plant&quot;. i.e. Stuffed Turkey.

[[Leaf of Cabbage|Leaves of Cabbage]] are created by splitting up a Cabbage Head; each Cabbage Head will produce 6 leaves of the same type as the head.

== Cabbages  ==
The specific type of Cabbage Head is dependent on the tier of the Field it was harvested from (see [[Agriculture]] for more on field tiers).
