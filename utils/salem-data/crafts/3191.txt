==About==

This is an inventory object when crafted, but must be burnt in a Kiln to become a [[Crucible]].  The Crucible is a liftable structure and will appear over the player's head when removed from the kiln.  It must then be carried or put in a sled or cart to transport to its final location.

It takes 2 hours to bake a Crucible in a Kiln, but if the Kiln is filled to full, there is more then enough fuel to do so without checking back and refueling.</text>
