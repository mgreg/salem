==About==

Can be be found rarely when Rummaging in a [[Squirrel Stash]] or from a [[Walnut Tree]], which aren't found in the wild and must be planted from cuttings.

Can be cracked open with [[Any Stone]] by right clicking on the Walnut Shell while holding the stone in hand. When cracking a Walnut Shell there is a small chance of recieving a [[Horribly Rotten Walnut]] instead of a [[Walnut]].</text>
