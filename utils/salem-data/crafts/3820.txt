==About==

Bonemeal can be made from [[Bone]]s or [[Fish Skeleton]]s in a Grindstone or a Copper Grinder.  Grinding a [[Darkenbone]] will create [[Darkenbonemeal]].

'''Ferilizer Uses:'''

Each field will use 0.60kg Bonemeal per application.

Fertilizer 1 = First fertilizer to be used on field since seeds were planted; for each fertilizer you used on the field (regardless of which kind) add that to determine which fertilizer # you're adding; Example: If you put (1) wood chip on the field, then used this fertilizer, you would get fertilizer 2 as the 2nd fertilizer used on the field.

==Formula==

The formula follows '''30/sqrt(x)''', so:

Fertilizer 1: +30 Speed, +30 Plenty

Fertilizer 2: +21 Speed, +21 Plenty

Fertilizer 3: +17 Speed, +17 Plenty

Fertilizer 4: +15 Speed, +15 Plenty

Fertilizer 5: +13 Speed, +13 Plenty

Fertilizer 6: +12 Speed, +12 Plenty

Fertilizer 7: +11 Speed, +11 Plenty

Fertilizer 8: +10 Speed, +10 Plenty

Fertilizer 9: +10 Speed, +10 Plenty

Fertilizer 10: +9 Speed, +9 Plenty

TOTAL GAIN IN 10 USES: +148 Speed, +148 Plenty</text>
