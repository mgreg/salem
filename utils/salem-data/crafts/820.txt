==About==

Green Bell Peppers and [[Red Bell Peppers]] are plants which can only be raised in a [[Gardening Pot]]. If you do not already have a pepper plant to place in a pot you will instead need to plant a [[New World Gourd]] which has a random chance of resulting in either [[Baby Bear]] (a type of pumpkin), Green Bell Peppers or [[Red Bell Peppers]].

They have good restore values when used as a variable ingredient, but are awful to eat as a gluttony food themselves.
