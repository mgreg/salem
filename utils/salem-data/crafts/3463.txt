==About==

The Candy Gang Candy Cane is a club type weapon which cannot be crafted.  It is one of several weapons that can be randomly found in the [[Mystery Weapon Chest]] available in the Salem store.

This weapon can actually be eaten!  Cannot be Gluttoned though. 

Can be hung on a wall.
