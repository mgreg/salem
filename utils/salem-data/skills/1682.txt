==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Blood is a juice of rarest quality.&quot;

''-Johann Wolfgang von Goethe, Faust

A pilgrim not skilled in the ways of Field Dressing is want and apt to make nothing but a mess where once was a fine carcass. Field Dressing allows you to extract cuts of meat from dead animals.

==About==

This is one if the first of several butchery skills that allow the player to extract extra meat from a carcass.  This skill increases the number of cuts obtained.</text>
