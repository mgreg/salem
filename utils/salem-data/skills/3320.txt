==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Till the monster stirred, that demon, that fiend Grendel who haunted the moors, the wild Marches, and made his home in a hell.&quot;''

''- Beowulf, circa 1000

While many make a habit of running or fleeing from the darkness, there are a few who choose to venture into it.  Monster Hunting allows a pilgrim to both engage and harvest the more fearsome of creatures.

==About==

Allows you to initiate combat with, and more importantly, harvest, darkness creatures like Argopelters and March Hares.

NOTE: If you don't have this skill, they can still initiate combat with you, and you can kill them, but you can't butcher them.</text>
