==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Even a blind squirrel sometimes finds a nut.&quot;

''-Proverb

Much of nature's most bountiful harvest comes clad in wooden armor.  Nuts &amp; Seeds allows you to find more nuts when foraging.

==About==

Increases frequency of [[Stray Chestnut]] and also increases the number of [[Myrtle Oak Acorn]]s, [[Pinecone]]s, and [[Hickory Nut]]s picked from trees.</text>
