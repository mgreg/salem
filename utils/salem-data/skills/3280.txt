==In-Game Text==

''&quot;I see thou hast good boots on, which are well blacked. &quot;said [the soldier] to the huntsmans; &quot;but if thou hadst to travel about as I have, they would not last long.  Look at mine, they are of buffalo-leather, and have been worn for a long time, but in them I can go through thick and thin.&quot;

''-The Boots of Buffalo Leather, Traditional German Folk Tale

If shoes tell a tale of where a man has been.  Then a Cobbler crafts the future of where a man may go.  Cobbling unlocks the ability to craft the finer footwear.
