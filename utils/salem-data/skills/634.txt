==In-Game Text==

''Whoe'er has gone thro' London street,

''Has seen a butcher gazing at his meat.

''And how he keeps

''Gloating upon a sheep's

''Or bullock's personals, as if his own;

''How he admires his halves

''And quarters-and his calves,

''As if in truth upon his own legs grown.

''-Thomas Hold, A Butcher

A Pilgrim versed in the ways of the butcher is able to extract more useful meat from animal carcasses. Butchery allows you to cut steaks.

==About==

This is one of several skills that allow the player to increase the amount of meat obtained from a carcass.  Butchery increases the number of steaks obtained.</text>
