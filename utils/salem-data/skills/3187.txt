==In-Game Text==

''&quot;The clay holds the blow-pipe when it is put into the furnace, whereas the shield preserves the eyes of the glass-maker from the fire.  All this having been carried out in order, the glass-makers bring the work to completion.&quot;''

''- Georgius Agricola, de Re Metallica

Having mastered the most primature arts of clay and iron, the intrepid student and aspiring alchemis may then proceed further on, in developing a more expanded understanding of the solidified juices, by mastering the arts of Ceramics &amp; Crucibles. Ceramics &amp; Crucibles allows you to create crucibles, by which glass bottles can be blown from panes.
