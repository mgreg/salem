==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Though blood be the best sauce for victory, yet must it not be more than the meat.&quot;

''-Thomas Fuller, The History of the Holy War

Grown and matured in the free widths of the great outdoors, the wild meat of the forest is an excellent source of nutrition. Game Meats allows you to extract extra cuts of meat from wild animal carcasses, and gives you access to some delicious free range recipes.

==About==

This is one of several skills that allow the player to increase the amount of meat obtained from a carcass. Game Meats increases the number of cuts obtained.</text>
