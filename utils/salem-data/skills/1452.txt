==In-Game Text==

''&quot;I am the Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the ending, saith the Lord, which is, and which was, and which is to come, the Almighty.&quot;

''-Revelation 1:8, King James' Bible

It will eventually come to dawn on the young Pilgrim that further advancement within any given field or profession will require a certain command of the written language.  Literacy allows you to read books, to write on signs and parchments, and also increases your inspiration rate.

==About==

Makes you recover Inspiration at an additional 0.1/second and unlocks the additional learning skills.  Literacy also opens up the ability to create a [[Wooden Sign]] which allows you to communicate with other players as well as read signs that other players have left around [[Salem]].</text>
