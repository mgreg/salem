==In-Game Text==

''&quot;And they said one to another, Go to, let us make brick, and burn them throughly.  And they had brick for stone, and slime had they for morter.&quot;

''-Genesis 11:3, King James' Bible

The permanent and lasting settlement will eventually require materials more durable against the winds and weathers than simple wood.  Masonry allows you to lay bricks, and to build brick ovens.
