Carolo Fracassato, I had set on foot in order to become more certain about the membranous substance of the lungs.&quot;

==About==

This skills increases the chances of harvesting things other than meat off an animal, such as [[Stringy Sinew]] or [[Whispering Snake Skull]].</text>
