==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Beside yon straggling fence that skirts the way

''With blossom'd furze unprofitably gay,

''There, in his noisy mansion, skill'd to rule,

''The village master taught his little school;&quot;

''-Oliver Goldsmith, The Village Schoolmaster

Having acquired for himself a plot of land to call his own, the wise Pilgrim strives next to defend the land from other comers.  Simple Fences allows you to construct sturdy fences to keep intruders out.

==About==

To make a fence, build a corner post, then right-click on the post to build in a cardinal direction.

To build a gate, leave a 2 square gap between two corner posts and right-click on a post to enable the gate option.</text>
