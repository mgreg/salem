==In-Game Text==

''&quot;A great flame follows a little spark.&quot;''

''- Dante, The Divine Comedy Canto 1 line 34, 1321

Torches allows a pilgrim with a claim to construct defensive TorchPosts whose illumination can help defend any Homestead or Town from the evil deeds of their neighbors.  It also allows for the construction of a simple torch which allows for the transportation of fire.
