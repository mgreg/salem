==In-Game Text==

''&quot;[All fluids] are water modified by a certain admixture, the nature of which determines their flavour.&quot;

''-Aristotle, Meteorlogy, 350 B.C.

The steam distiller sometimes lets off a sweet aroma, other times a pungent one.  Either way, each drop it produces is as good as liquid gold to a capable alchemist.

==About==

The [[Steam Distiller]] is used to extract a liquid essence from an item and condense it into a vial.

At this time, it is only used to produce [[Rose Otto]] from fresh [[Red Rose]]s for the making of [[Fine Leather]].  [[Dehydrated Rose]] is a nice byproduct of this process, but it must be retrieved from the distiller within two hours or it will disintegrate.</text>
