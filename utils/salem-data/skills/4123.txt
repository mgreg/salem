==In-Game Text==

''&quot;How well the skillful gard'ner drew&lt;br&gt;
''Of flow'rs and herbs this dial new,&lt;br&gt;
''Where from above the milder sun&lt;br&gt;
''Does through a fragrant zodiac run;&lt;br&gt;
''And as it works, th' industrious bee&lt;br&gt;
''Computes its time as well as we.&lt;br&gt;
''How could such sweet and wholesome hours&lt;br&gt;
''Be reckon'd but with herbs and flow'rs!&quot;

''-Andrew Marvell, The Garden

Nothing warms the heart like potted flowers decorating a homestead, and now you too can cultivate beauty of your very own!  Floriculture allows for the planting of flowers in your gardening pots.

==About==

Red Roses, Sunny Sunflowers, and Devil's Worts require Floriculture to be planted in a pot.</text>
