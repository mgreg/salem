==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Frugality may be termed the daughter of Prudence, the sister of Temperance and the parent of Liberty.&quot;

-Samuel Johnson, The Rambler

A good butcher is not only measured by the quantity of his cuts, but also by his ability to bring home more meat in fewer slices.  Butcher's Thrift allows you to extract slabs of meat from larger game.

==About==

This is one of several skills that allow the player to increase the amount of meat obtained from a carcass. This skill allows a player to harvest Slab of Meat from Bears and Deer.</text>
