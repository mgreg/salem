==In-Game Text==

''&quot;In a country well governed, poverty is something to be ashamed of.  In a country badly goverened, wealth is something to be ashamed of.&quot;

''- Confucius

A relief for those too busy, rich, or entitled to be bothered shaking a customer's hand.  Mercantilism allows for the owning &amp; maintaining of a public stall.

==About==

You can only own one stall per character that has Mercantilism.  Player Stalls are located in Boston, to the East, between the statue and the shore.</text>
