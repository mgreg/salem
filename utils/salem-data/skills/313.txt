==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Whereas the late King James the Second by the Assistance of diverse evill Councellors Judges and Ministers imployed by him did endeavour to subvert and extierpate the Protestant Religion and the Lawes and Liberties of this Kingdome ... the Subjects which are Protestants may have Arms for their Defence suitable to their Conditions and as allowed by Law.&quot;

''-Bill of Rights 1689

It is an ancient and established right of Englishmen to bear arms in defense of life, property, King and Country.  Firearms allows you the means to exercise that right.

==About==

The Firearms skill is needed in order to wield [[Flintlock Musket]], [[Flintlock Pistol]], [[Founding Father's Arquebus]], or [[Redcoat's Musket]].

All such weapons require [[Musket Balls]] and [[Gunpowder]].</text>
