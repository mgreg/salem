==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Methinks it is a token of healthy and gentle characteristics, when women of high thoughts and accomplishments love to sew; especially as they are never more at home with their own hearts then while to occupied.&quot;

''-Nathaniel Hawthorne, The Marble Faun

Needle and thread can help both mend worn-out clothes, as well as, in the hands of the accomplished practitioner, create new ones. Sewing allows you access to a small range of clothes.
