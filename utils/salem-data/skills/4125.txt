==In-Game Text==

''&quot;In nothing do men more nearly approach the gods than in giving health to men.&quot;

''-Cicero

The studies of the learned inevitably reflect back into the human condition, and in so doing, ways in which the condition can be bettered.  Too often are Pilgrims hampered by the dangers around them, making a basic understanding of treating wounds necessary for civilization to thrive.  Rudimentary Triage represents the beginnings of medical knowledge and teaches settlers how to bandage wounds and tend broken bones.
