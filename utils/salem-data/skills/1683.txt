==In-Game Text==

''&quot;But perhaps I had better pass over our divines in silence and not stir this pool or touch this fair but unsavory plant.&quot;

''-Erasmus of Potterdam, The Praise of Folly

Foraging for supplies can be a difficult endeavor under the best of circumstances, but in a strange and wild land unfamiliar to the would-be woodsman the difficulty of this most critical art of survival can approach the Herculean.  Foraging allows you your first insights into the wild plant-life of the New World.

==About==

*This skill enables you to [[Forage]] some basic herbs which is essential for your survival in the New World.
 
*You can also use a special [[:Category:Movement|Movement]] called {{i|Forage}} that increases the number of items found in the wilderness.

*:However, leaving this skill on continuously drains [[Yellow Bile]] and costs [[Phlegm]] while moving.

*:This skill is a toggle: use once to start Foraging, use a second time (or activate another [[:Category:Movement|Movement]]) to stop Foraging.</text>
