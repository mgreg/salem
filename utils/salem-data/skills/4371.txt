==In-Game Text==

''Noxiae poena par esto.

(Let the punishment fit the offense.)

''Marcus Tullius Cicero, 40 B.C.

As a self-rectifying machine, society has grown to enact its own punishments on wrong-doers in a variety of situations.  Sanctioned Mortification instructs Pilgrims on the proper method of enacting such social justice by teaching the finer points of public shaming.  You will be able to interact with criminals in the stocks now to exact the appropriate amount of punishment.

==About==

Allows you to throw tomatoes at criminals in the stocks and allows you to whip them with a stall or store purchased whip.

Tomatoes add a [[Full and Fed Up]] debuff to the criminal, while whipping permanently removes humors, which then have to be restored with Gluttony.</text>
