==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Yet we have good hope, that what is here presented, and hath been by the Convocations of both Provinces with great diligence examined and approved; will be also well accepted and approved by all sober, peaceable, and truly conscientious Sons of the Church of England.&quot;

''- Book of Common Prayer, 1662

In light of the recent schisms within both the Universal Church, and the Church of England herself, never before has the formal study of true religion seemed a more prudent and important task. Theology is necessary to Construct Churches, and also allows you to create the garments of liturgy.
