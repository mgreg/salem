==In-Game Text==

''&quot;I shall only instance in one delight more, the most natural and best-natured of all others, a perpetual companion of the husbandman; and that is, the satisfaction of looking round about him, and seeing nothing but the effects and improvements of his own art and diligence; to be always gathering of some fruies of it, and at the same time to behold others ripening, and others budding; to see all his fields and gardens covered with the beauteous creatures of his own industry; and to see, like God, that all his works are good.&quot;

''-Abraham Cowley, Of Agriculture

Truly a Pilgrim who has attained the Green Thumb has surpassed the study of gardening and is now attuned to the art of nature's own rhythm.  He is an avatar of creation, a sage of seeds, and a grower of all things.  A Green Thumb coaxes plants into ripening faster than ever before!

==About==

Drastically speeds up the rate at which pots mature.</text>
