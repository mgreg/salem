==In-Game Text==

''&quot;I am... a mushroom on whom the dew of heaven drops now and then.&quot;

''-John Ford, The Broken Heart

Too few know the treasures hidden beneath log and rock, the intricacies of the mycellium patterns working their way over the soil to produce a bounty unlike anything in the plant world.  Fungiculture provides the proper instruction for settlers to grow mushrooms.

==About==

Sugar caps, Lavender Blewits, Lobster Mushrooms, Waxing Toadstools, Witch's Caps, and Yellow Morels, require Fungiculture to be planted in a pot.</text>
