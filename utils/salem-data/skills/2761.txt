==In-Game Text==

''&quot;It has black and white plumage, wrinkled skin on its neck, and on the top of its head a fleshy crest which, when the cock gets angry, swells up and covers its whole snout; on some of them that crest is a russet colour mixed with bluish purple.&quot;

''-Bartolomeo Scappi, Opera dell'arte del cucinare, 1570

Many are the peculiar lifeforms which in the time of the great flood left Noah's ark to find terra firma in this New World, and few among them are more peculiar than the Turkey.  Turkey Farming allows you to construct Turkey Coops, which can be used to breed Turkeys.

==About==

Turkey Farming allows characters to build [[Turkey Coop]]s, which when populated with a few wild [[Turkey]]s will allow characters to breed Domestic Turkeys and thus acquire large sums of [[Turkey Feathers]], [[Turkey Egg]]s, and other useful items.

Remember to stock the Coop with food ''before'' adding the initial Turkeys, as they will die very quickly if left in the inventory or in a Coop without any food.</text>
