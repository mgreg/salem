==In-Game Text==

''&quot;It appears that God hath appoynted for a super-natural signe of the monstruous impietie of the Witches that the water shal refuse to receive them in her bosom.&quot;

''-King James I, Daemonologie

Everywhere one goes one finds the landscape of the New World strewn and dotted with brooks, fords, streams, lakes and eddies.  From the icy cold of Hudson's Bay to the blessed warmth of the Summer Isles and beyond, Swimming grants the ability to move in and through water.

==About==

To use it, select the Adventure Menu button and then click on the Activate Swimming button.  Be sure to turn Swimming off whenever not immediately using it to prevent accidental Swimming.  Swimming uses a '''LOT''' of Phlegm and is a common cause of knock outs.</text>
