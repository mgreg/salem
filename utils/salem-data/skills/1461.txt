==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Afterwards he lifteth the Beam upon Tressels by the help of a Pully, fasteneth it with Cramp-irons and marketh it out with a Line.  Then he frameth the Walls together, and fasteneth the great Pieces with Pins.&quot;
 
''-John Amos Comenius, Orbis Sensualium Pictus, The Visible World in Pictures, 1658

The completion of certain projects of the higher order also require more cultured feats of woodworking.  Joinery &amp; Finish allows you to build a Carpenter's Bench and an Oiling Trough, to lay down timber Piles for drying, and to properly plane and level boards.
