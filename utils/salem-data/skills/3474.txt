==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Though a tree grows so high, the falling leaves return to the root.&quot;

''- Malay Proverb

Careless Pilgrims often overlook the simple treasures of a tree.  Acute Tree Harvesting increases the quantity and quality of resources obtained from trees.

==About==

Allows you to gather more nuts, leaves, and bark from trees than you could before.  Also gives better chance of getting a [[Perfect Autumn Leaf]] from Maple trees.</text>
