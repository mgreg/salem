==In-Game Text==

''&quot;Through primrose tufts, in that green bower,

''The periwinkle trailed its wreaths;

''And 'tis my faith that every flower

''Enjoys the air it breathes.

''-William Wordsworth, Lines Written in Early Spring

Nature abounds in opulence with wild and beautiful things ripe for the plucking.  Flowers &amp; Berries allows you increased successes when foraging for wild flowers and berries.

==About==

Also allows players to Uproot bushes and carry them to a new place to plant.  Bushes can't be placed in sleds or carried in any other way but above the head.  Thorn Bushes will do constant Blood damage to the player while being carried.</text>
