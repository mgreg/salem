==In-Game Text==

''&quot;He made a pit, and digged it, and is fallen into the ditch which he made.&quot;

''-Psalm 7:15, King James' Bible

While fertile and rich as such, the soil of the New World will nevertheless not yield any fruit or treasure unless adequate payment in sweat and blood has first been rendered unto it.  Labouring allows you to carry more dug out soil, and reduces the Phlegm cost of digging.
